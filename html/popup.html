WHALEVAULT
Graphene Cross-Chain Key Store

WhaleVault is a better, safer cross-chain way to access all your graphene accounts from the browser.

First off, you'll need a "Master Password", required to access any of your connected graphene accounts.

Warning: WhaleVault is not meant to replace permanent out-of-browser cold storage such as the KeePass Password Safe (keepass.info).

Set up

Enter the base chain and account name, and either (1) your master password, OR (2) the private keys you wish to link to the account. Master passwords are used only to auto-generate your private keys, and are NOT SAVED. You may add or remove accounts or individual keys at any time.

About

WhaleVault is a multi-chain fork by @alexpmorris of the Steem Keychain browser extension. Steem Keychain was originally created by @yabapmatt, developed by @stoodkev, and funded by @aggroed. Many thanks to them for creating a great template upon which to build WhaleVault!
