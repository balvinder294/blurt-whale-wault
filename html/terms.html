WHALEVAULT
Graphene Cross-Chain Key Store
WhaleVault is a better, safer cross-chain way to access all your graphene accounts from the browser.

First off, you'll need a "Master Password", required to access any of your connected graphene accounts.

Warning: WhaleVault is not meant to replace permanent out-of-browser cold storage such as the KeePass Password Safe (keepass.info).
WhaleVault is a chrome extension designed for managing multiple graphene blockchain accounts, and allows websites to authenticate identify and sign transactions with your explicit permission, and without ever having to directly expose your private keys to any particular website directly. Any materials such as tutorials, code, written documentation and other content related to WhaleVault also falls under this agreement.

Users are always in control of their own digital assets (such as accounts, keypairs, and tokens) via popup boxes enabling the user to Accept or Deny each transaction, information request, and additional requests. Certain transactions can also be white-listed on a site-by-site basis.
